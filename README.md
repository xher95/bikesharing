# NYC Citi Bike Analysis
This is a summary of the analytic work that I did with the NYC Citi Bike data.
## Introduction
The modern Bike sharing business is a new emergent business model that is made possible by augmenting bike rental business with modern technology. Customers can access wireless connected bicycles anywhere easily and conveniently. Bicycle technology itself can also be augmented with modern enhancements. Enhancements may include: theft prevention, electric motors, rent and return anywhere, and big data and sensor tracking, the possibilities are endless. The demand for modern bike rental service had been growing tremendously across the globe.
